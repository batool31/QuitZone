Your Next Health Milestones

Circulation Improves

2 weeks after quitting

Walking becomes easier as circulation improves. Lung function begins to improve.

Breathing Improvement

1-3 months after quitting

Your lung function increases up to 30%. Coughing and shortness of breath decreases.

Heart Disease Risk Drops

1 year after quitting

Your risk of coronary heart disease is about half that of a smoker's.

Stroke Risk Reduced

2-5 years after quitting

Your risk of stroke is reduced to that of a nonsmoker 2-5 years after quitting.

Cancer Risk Decreases

10 years after quitting

Your risk of lung cancer falls to about half that of a smoker. Risk of other cancers decreases.
